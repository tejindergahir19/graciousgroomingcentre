Communication Skills

Special emphasis is provided to English as it is the global language of opportunity. Remaining in line with a World-class curriculum, we emphasize upon English as a viable medium of communication and as dedicated subject in itself. Being proficient in English is a crucial life skill, which aids a child with the ability to move ahead in further education. English as an important learning area focuses on language, literacy and basic concepts of the world, which is useful for forming major aspects of a child’s development process. English Language and literacy focuses on language skills development and using language for communicating and learning. English is a language that provides children with the opportunity to enhance language skills and broadens their conceptual understanding of the world.
